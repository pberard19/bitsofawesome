My Coding Philosophy

I am an advocater of the Keep It Simple, Silly (KISS) method. Not all problems have simple solutions, but the mark of a great programmer is the ability to convey the solution in a simple manner so others can understand it. If you win the lottery tomorrow, will others be able to maintain your code? Will you be able to read your own code in a year? 6 months from now? 3 weeks from now?
Good comments, clear code, and well organized programs help me sleep at night.
